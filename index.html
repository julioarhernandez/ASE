Frequently Asked Question

To assist you in making an informed choice, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions about our electrical services. If you have additional questions or require further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our team. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we're here to help.

  • Do You Offer Emergency Services for Residential Clients?

    Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency electrical services for residential clients to address urgent issues promptly and ensure your family's safety.

  • What Is the Average Cost of Upgrading an Electrical Panel in South Florida?

    The cost of upgrading an electrical panel can vary depending on the specific requirements of your home. Factors like the size of the panel, the complexity of the installation, and any necessary permits can influence the cost. We offer free estimates to give you a more accurate assessment.

  • Are Your Electricians Licensed and Insured?

    Yes, our team of electricians is fully licensed and insured, ensuring that you receive professional and reliable service that meets all safety and regulatory standards.

  • What Type of Surge Protection Is Recommended for South Florida Homes?

    South Florida experiences frequent lightning storms, making whole-house surge protectors a wise investment. These devices provide comprehensive protection for your appliances and electronics.

  • Do You Offer Installation Services for Electric Vehicle Charging Stations?

    Yes, we specialize in the installation of electric vehicle (EV) charging stations. We can help you set up a convenient and efficient charging solution for your EV to keep you on the road without interruption.
